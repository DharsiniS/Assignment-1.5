1. Explain hadoop in layman's term

	Hadoop makes it possible to run applications on systems with numerous hardwares and large amount of data.
	Two major components of Hadoop are- Mapreduce and HDFS.
	HDFS is used for storage while Mapreduce is used for processing.

2. Explain the components of Hadoop framework

	There are two components in Hadoop framework:
	HDFS- This deals with the storage purpose.
	MapReduce-This deals with the processing purpose.
